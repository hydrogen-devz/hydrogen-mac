## 🐛 Common Issues and Fixes

### ❌ macOS blocks Hydrogen with "unidentified developer" warning
**Fix:** Go to **System Preferences** → Security & Privacy → General → click **Open Anyway** next to the Hydrogen entry. On macOS Sequoia, this option appears under **System Settings** → Privacy & Security → scroll to the bottom.

---

### ❌ Hydrogen UI doesn't appear after injection
**Fix:** Check that both **Accessibility** and **Screen Recording** permissions are granted to Hydrogen in System Preferences → Privacy & Security. If permissions are already granted, try toggling them off and back on, then relaunch Hydrogen.

---

### ❌ "Injection failed" after a Roblox update
**Fix:** Run the Terminal update command immediately. Roblox client updates frequently break injection offsets — an outdated Hydrogen build will always fail after a patch. This is the most common cause of injection failures by far.

---

### ❌ Key verification loops or never completes
**Fix:** Switch to a different network (e.g., mobile hotspot instead of Wi-Fi), disable your VPN if active, and retry. If you are a verified community member on the **hydrogen mac discord** server, you may be eligible for the adless (keyless) build which skips this step entirely.

---

### ❌ Script Hub loads blank on Android
**Fix:** The Script Hub requires an active internet connection. Clear Hydrogen's app cache under **Settings** → Apps → Hydrogen → Storage → Clear Cache, then reopen the app.

---

### ❌ Hydrogen crashes immediately on Intel Mac after update
**Fix:** The installer may have downloaded an ARM binary by mistake. Re-run the Terminal installer — it auto-detects your CPU architecture and downloads the correct build.
